Easy Pancakes!

Description

Light and fluffy! Who can resist pancakes especially when they are quick and easy to prepare. This recipe allows you to make this magical breakfast dish anytime. This recipe doesn't require much thought to prepare and you can dress it up with any additional toppings you see fit. I especially enjoy my pancakes with blueberries and confectionery sugar!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled gridled or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
